About me

Hi, my name is Clare McAuliffe.

I'm the youngest of four and some of my favorite memories are family dinners. Ever since I was little I loved to bake. It wasn't until high school that I really started cooking. I tore my acl, mcl, and minscus and had to take a year off of soccer. With so much free time I decided to spend it working and having fun in my kitchen. This page is meant to give some insight to some of my favorite bloggers as well as some go to recipes that my family and I now consider staples.

On my instagram you can see a wide variety of foods I've made. I try to eat healthy for the most part but remain a firm believer of balance in everything!

Check out my instagram @eatsbycfm for some more creations!
