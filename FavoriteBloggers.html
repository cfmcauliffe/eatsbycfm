My Favorite Bloggers

Gathered Nurtition

Created and lead by Mia Zarlengo, she is a registered dietitian, lover of all things food and nutrition, coffee obsessed, recipe developer based in Chicago. She focusses on creating accessible yet healthy recipes, finding simplicity in the kitchen, as well as topics on women’s hormonal health.

Check her out!

Modern Proper

The Modern Proper materialized as 2 friends, Holly Erickson and Natalie Mortimer, found themselves with a common desire to share recipes and hospitality ideas. We began taking photos and writing stories around our favorite foods and sharing them online. Over time, we have discovered a wonderful community of like-minded people who love any opportunity to host their loved ones while eating a delicious meal.

Check them out!

Earthy Andy

EarthyAndy.com, by Andrea Hannemann, is designed to help you find answers and give guidance for your health concerns and become an expert on your body and your unique health. I hope this is a place of support and knowledge where you can learn how to unveil the best version of you! Here you will find meal plans, health tips, recipes, health guidance, talk, family life, exercise info, skin and hair tips all while having a little fun! This is not a quick-fix diet. This is a lifestyle that can improve your long-term quality of life. This is a holistic approach - taking a look at mind, body, and soul.

Check her out!

Ambitious Kitchen

Monique, a Chicago based food blogger, first developed her love for cooking with her Puerto Rican mom at a young age. On her blog you will be sure to find a balanced mix of globally-inspired recipes with a healthy twist, baking recipes that come straight from my heart, and of course, a healthy dose of real talk.

Check her out!

Love & Lemons

Jeanine created Love and Lemons to celebrate how nourishing, fun, and delicious cooking with seasonal fruits and vegetables can be. Her recipes are made ot inspire people to a new vegetable, or try cooking a familiar vegetable in a new way. Although a vegitarian based food blog you’ll find a host of inventive and tasty recipes for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Check her out!

Pinch of Yum

Lindsey started her blog from a casual hobby of cooking on the weekends. This turned into a platform that reaches millions of people. She idea is to create fresh, approachable, and exciting meals to nourish. Based in Saint Paul she wants to help inspire people to make floavraful food.

Check her out!

Half Baked Harvest

Tieghan, the creator behind this blog, was originally inspired by the people and places she loves most. Some of her recipes more on the decadent side, pack a ton of flavor. She bases her recipes to live simply, eat seasonally, and cook with whole foods. Inspiring to create a love for amazing food, as well as the courage to try something new!

Check her out!

Gimme Soe Oven

Created by Ali, she started this as a side hobby in 2009. Believing some of the best memories are created in the kitchen, she works hard to inspire people to create home cooked meals that bring everyone together. Most of her recipes are pretty simple and easy to make no matter the size or complexity of one's kitchen.

Check her out!

Minimalist Baker

Minimalist Baker celebrates simple cooking by sharing recipes that require 10 ingredients or less, 1 bowl, or 30 minute or less to prepare. Having tons of vegan and vegitarian options, this blog is made for many different types of eaters. Dana Shultz, the main creator, wanted to give people recipes that have whole ingrediantes, require minimal equipment, few ingrediantes, and a short amount of time.

Check her out!
